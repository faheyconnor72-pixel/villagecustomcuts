Our Story

A Neighborhood Barbershop That Takes Pride in Every Cut.

Village Custom Cuts has been serving Croton-on-Hudson for over a decade. We're not a chain — we're your neighbors. Every barber here has a name you'll remember and a craft you'll feel.

From classic tapers to tight fades and hot towel shaves, we bring precision and care to every chair, every time. Walk in anytime, or book your spot online.
